Who I am

Storytelling meets
strategy.

I am an Audiovisual Content Producer, Videographer, and Social Media Specialist with over 13 years of experience creating high-impact content for professional sports, institutions, and digital platforms.

My career began in 2007 in photography, quickly expanding into live event coverage, concert photography, and commercial work. From 2015 to 2022, I spent seven years at Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense — one of Brazil's leading football clubs — transforming their YouTube channel from a 45K-subscriber platform into a 900K+ subscriber ecosystem.

In 2023 I relocated to Europe — first Italy, then Ireland. Shortly after arriving in Dublin, I joined Erin College, a leading English language school in central Dublin, as Social Media Specialist. Over ten months I managed the school's full digital presence: content production, multi-platform social media, influencer collaboration targeting the Mongolian student market, a student experience podcast series, WordPress management, and monthly campaign planning — all within a fast-paced, multilingual, international environment.

This Irish experience deepened my understanding of cross-cultural digital communication and gave me first-hand knowledge of the local professional landscape. I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Social Communication — Advertising and Marketing. My approach combines storytelling instinct, technical precision, and data-driven editorial thinking.

Case Study

GrêmioTV —
900K subscribers.

GrêmioTV is the official digital media platform of Grêmio Foot-Ball Porto Alegrense, one of Brazil's most traditional football clubs with nearly 10 million supporters. When I joined in December 2015, the channel had approximately 45,000 subscribers and operated primarily as a supplementary content platform.

Over seven years, I contributed to transforming it into a structured, brand-driven digital media operation — scaling to 900,000+ subscribers and consolidating it as the club's primary owned media asset through long-term consistency, format innovation, and visual standardisation.

Case Study

Erin College —
Social Media in Dublin.

Erin College is a leading English language school in central Dublin, accredited by Irish government education bodies and serving a global student community from dozens of countries. From January to October 2024, I worked as Social Media Specialist across the school's Dublin and Mountjoy Street campuses.

In this role I was responsible for the school's full digital presence — from content planning and production to website management and campaign execution — in a fast-paced, multicultural and multilingual environment. Every piece of content had to connect authentically with an international audience while supporting enrollment and brand visibility objectives.
